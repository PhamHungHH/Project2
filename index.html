Experiences & Activities

Best Time to Visit

Winter Wonderland

December - March

Experience the magic of Finnish winter with snow-covered landscapes, northern lights, and winter activities. Temperatures range from -5°C to -30°C.

  • Aurora Borealis viewing
  • Husky and reindeer safaris
  • Ice fishing and skating
  • Santa Claus Village in Rovaniemi
  • Ski resorts in Lapland

Spring Revival

April - May

Witness nature waking up with melting snow, flowing rapids, and migrating birds. Temperatures vary from 0°C to 15°C.

  • Witnessing the awakening of nature
  • Longer daylight hours
  • Hiking and birdwatching
  • Spring festivals
  • Fewer tourists and better prices

Midnight Sun

June - August

Experience the magical midnight sun with endless daylight. Warm temperatures between 15°C to 25°C make this the most popular season.

  • Midnight sun phenomenon
  • Swimming in lakes and the Baltic Sea
  • Cottage life and sauna experiences
  • Midsummer celebration (Juhannus)
  • Vibrant cities and outdoor cafés

Autumn Colors

September - November

Witness "ruska" - the spectacular autumn foliage. Temperatures cool from 15°C to near freezing with potential early snow.

  • Spectacular autumn colors (ruska)
  • Mushroom and berry picking
  • Early northern lights
  • Peaceful hiking trails
  • Cozy cafés and cultural events

Travel Information

Getting There

Finland is well-connected with international flights to Helsinki-Vantaa Airport. From there, domestic flights, trains, and buses connect to major cities. EU citizens can enter with just an ID card.

Getting Around

Finland has an excellent transportation system with trains, buses, and domestic flights. In cities, public transport is efficient. Renting a car is recommended for exploring remote areas.

Language

Finnish and Swedish are the official languages, but English is widely spoken, especially in tourist areas, hotels, and restaurants.

Currency

The currency is Euro (€). Credit cards are widely accepted, even for small purchases. ATMs are readily available in cities and towns.
